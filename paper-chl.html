The Research

This study tackles a critical question in precision agriculture: can we accurately map crop chlorophyll from space? We tested five machine learning regression algorithms (KRR, GPR, PLSR, LSLR, and Neural Networks) across three distinct scenarios, including standalone satellite imagery, UAV-satellite data fusion, and a hybrid PROSAIL radiative transfer model approach.

The results showed that fusing UAV data with satellite imagery significantly outperforms both standalone satellite and physics-based hybrid models. Using Kernel Ridge Regression (KRR) on RapidEye data fused with UAV measurements, we achieved R = 0.94, a substantial jump from R = 0.55 without fusion on Landsat 7.
